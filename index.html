Why do we need Motivational quotes in our life ?

Motivational quotes instill a sense of positivity in our life when nothing seems to work. When you feel like you are doomed to fail and there's no one coming to save you, there's no one you could talk to. When your parents, friends, co-workers do not understand your ambitions or the unique career journey you want to embark on. When there's a lack of faith being shown in you. When you get bullied in school. When you are unattractive, have a bad voice or have an unfortunate appearance. When not even a single hair on your body feels the sensation of excitement. When hope seems like a luxury. During all these situations, motivational quotes rescue you from the abyss of self-loathing, depression and complete loss of confidence.
